Sleep Wellness

Discover healthy habits and tools to improve your sleep quality and mental well-being.

Why Sleep Matters

Sleep is essential for mental clarity, emotional balance, and overall health. Poor sleep can worsen anxiety, depression, and stress levels.

Tips for Better Sleep

  • Stick to a consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Reduce caffeine and heavy meals at night
  • Keep your sleep environment dark and quiet

Techniques to Improve Sleep

  • Deep breathing and meditation
  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Guided imagery
  • CBT for insomnia
