Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Change your thoughts, change your life.

What is CBT?

CBT is a proven psychological therapy that helps you identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors. It‚Äôs based on the idea that how we think, feel, and act are interconnected.

How CBT Works

CBT focuses on present issues by helping you recognize distorted thinking and replace it with realistic thoughts. Sessions may include:

  • Identifying automatic negative thoughts
  • Challenging unhelpful beliefs
  • Practicing new behaviors

Popular CBT Techniques

  • Cognitive Restructuring
  • Exposure Therapy
  • Behavioral Activation
  • Thought Recording
  • Problem-Solving
